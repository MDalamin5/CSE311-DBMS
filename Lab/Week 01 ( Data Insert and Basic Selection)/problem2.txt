2. Creation of a New table named department_new
You have to create a table named department_new as per following requirements. A department has department_id, department_name, manager_id and location_id. The department_id, manager_id and location_id will be 4 digit, 6 digit and 4 digit integer (number) respectively. The department_name will be maximum 30 characters (type varchar).

answer:

create table department_new(
department_id int(4),
department_name varchar(30),
manager_id int(6),
location_id int(4)
);
